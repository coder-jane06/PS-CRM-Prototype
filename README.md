# PS-CRM: Smart Public Service Command Center
### Submission for India Innovates 2026

**PS-CRM** is a centralized digital platform that brings all citizen complaints, government departments, and field officers onto a single intelligent dashboard. Our system automates the entire grievance lifecycle—from filing to resolution—to eliminate manual backlogs and ensure administrative accountability.

---

## 🚀 The Vision
In India, over 2 crore grievances are filed annually, yet resolution rates remain below 60%. PS-CRM addresses the "Broken Cycle" by:

* **Eliminating Silos:** Bringing PWD, Jal Board, and Electricity departments onto a single dashboard to prevent complaints from falling through the cracks.
* **AI-Powered Efficiency:** Using NLP to auto-classify complaints and merge duplicates.
* **Real-Time Accountability:** Live tracking with auto-escalation to senior officers if resolution deadlines (SLAs) are breached.

---

## 📊 Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)

1. **AI That Predicts, Not Just Processes**
PS-CRM analyses patterns to predict infrastructure failures before they happen, enabling preventive action rather than damage control.

2. **Duplicate Complaint Merging**
500 citizens reporting the same pothole becomes one clean ticket, eliminating system overload and ensuring each issue is addressed once.

3. **WhatsApp-First Filing**
Citizens can file complaints directly via WhatsApp with no app download or registration barrier, ensuring maximum accessibility for all demographics.

4. **Officer-Level Accountability**
Field officers verify complaints on-site to eliminate false reports, while the system auto-escalates breached deadlines—a two-way accountability mechanism.

5. **Geographic Heatmaps for Smarter Decision-Making**
Live density overlays identify overwhelmed zones, while a second efficiency heatmap tracks regional resolution rates to guide resource deployment.

6. **Public Department Performance Dashboard**
Resolution rates, response times, and SLA compliance are publicly visible, creating institutional pressure to perform, not just process.

7. **Carbon Impact Tracking**
The system tags environment-linked complaints and tracks green resolutions, directly aligned with Smart City and net-zero mandates.
